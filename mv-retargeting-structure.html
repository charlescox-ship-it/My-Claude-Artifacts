Retargeting Campaign Structure

Example Quest: Be Extraordinary  ·  Two separate campaigns mapped to audience intent level

📺 WARM RETARGETING
Warm Audiences — Masterclass Re-engagement
Optimise for: Masterclass Registration
Strategic role: These audiences have engaged with Be Extraordinary content but haven't registered for the masterclass yet. They haven't had the full education and trust-building experience, so pushing a direct sale is premature. The job here is to get them to watch the masterclass — which does the selling for us.
👥 Audiences (Single Ad Set)
25% video viewers — from Be Extraordinary prospecting ads (short-form) or VSL ads
Link clickers — clicked ads leading to VSL page or masterclass registration page, did not register (60 days)
⛔ Exclusions
• 50%+ VSL video viewers
• Masterclass registrants
• Repeat site visitors (2+ visits, 30 days)
• Add to carts + checkout abandoners
• Existing members / purchasers
📋 Ad Set
Single Ad Set · Warm Audiences
Quest-specific creatives only (Be Extraordinary) · Video & banner formats
Outcomes Focus Testimonials Results in Advance Problem Agitation Humour / Pattern Interrupt
🎨 Creative Notes — Video & Banner Formats
All creative types can run as both video and static banner formats. Outcome-based videos are short, focused on the specific transformation this quest drives. Results in advance shows the outcome they can expect before registering, building desire. Problem agitation re-surfaces the pain point that makes this quest relevant. Humour and pattern interrupt help break through scroll fatigue on audiences who may have already seen prospecting ads. Teacher credibility builds trust for those who engaged but weren't yet convinced. Banners work particularly well for outcome messaging and testimonial pull-quotes.
→ Destination: Masterclass Registration Page
Same registration page as prospecting. Outcome-congruent with the ad that drove original engagement.
💳 HIGH INTENT RETARGETING
High Intent Audiences — Direct Sales
Optimise for: Purchase
Strategic role: These audiences have already been through the education — they've registered for or watched the masterclass, or have shown high purchase intent through cart/checkout behaviour. Sending them back to another masterclass is unnecessary friction. The job now is to remove the final barrier to purchase: handle objections, reinforce value, and create urgency.
👥 Audiences (Single Ad Set)
50%+ video viewers — from Be Extraordinary VSL ads (15+ minutes watched — substantial sales exposure)
Masterclass registrants — registered for Be Extraordinary masterclass, did not purchase
Repeat site visitors — visited Be Extraordinary pages 2+ times (30 days)
Add to carts + further down funnel — initiated checkout or abandoned at payment
⛔ Exclusions
• Existing members / purchasers
📋 Ad Set
Single Ad Set · High Intent Audiences
Quest + pathway-focused creatives (Be Extraordinary & Manifesting Collection) · Video & banner formats
Objection Handlers Pathway Expansion Testimonials Teacher Credibility ⭐ Offer-Led / Urgency
🎨 Creative Notes
Objection handlers address purchase barriers — price, time commitment, "will it work for me?". Pathway expansion ads introduce the broader collection value — what else is included beyond the featured quest, the full scope of the pathway. Testimonials focus on transformation outcomes. Teacher credibility reinforces authority and trust at the point of decision. Offer-led creative — payment plans, money-back guarantee, limited bonus — is exclusive to this campaign; these people are sold on the concept, the job is removing the final friction.
→ Destination: Quest Sales Page
Be Extraordinary-focused landing page selling the Manifesting Collection. Outcome-congruent. No masterclass re-registration required.

Retargeting Strategy: Key Principles

Two campaigns mapped to intent and journey — not one undifferentiated retargeting pool

📺 Warm Retargeting
✓ Audiences: 25% video viewers + LP visitors
✓ Exclude all high intent audiences + purchasers
✓ Optimise: purchase event
✓ Quest-specific creatives only
✓ Video & banner formats
✓ Destination: masterclass registration page
✓ Journey: ad → registration → masterclass → sales page → purchase
💳 High Intent Retargeting
✓ Audiences: 50%+ VSL viewers, MC registrants, repeat visitors, add to carts
✓ Exclude: purchasers only
✓ Optimise: purchase event
✓ Quest + pathway-focused creatives
✓ Offer-led / urgency creative exclusive to this campaign
✓ Destination: quest sales page (direct)
✓ Journey: ad → sales page → purchase
⚠️ Why Two Separate Campaigns — Not Two Ad Sets
Although both campaigns optimise for the same purchase event, the conversion journeys are fundamentally different. Warm retargeting routes traffic through a multi-step masterclass funnel before reaching checkout. High intent retargeting goes direct to the sales page. Meta builds its optimisation model around the full path to conversion — not just the final event — so combining these journeys in one campaign creates conflicting signals and degrades algorithm performance. Two separate campaigns keeps the learning clean and the budget allocation deliberate.
