CN1 review
Internal green-light session where ads, landing pages, email sequences and campaign setup are reviewed and signed off before any campaign launches. Nothing goes live without CN1 approval.
Testing campaign (7 days)
Launches the day after CN1. Static ads only, ABO, 3 ads per ad set optimised for link clicks. Budget $50–75 per variant (~$450–675 per quest). Results reviewed on day 7 — winning outcomes and angles inform creative production priorities.
Prospecting (always on)
Launches 8 days post-CN1, informed by testing signals. CBO, broad + ASC audiences, conversion-optimised. 3 outcome-based ad sets with 5–10 diverse creatives each. Runs continuously — all campaigns continue into Q2 and beyond.
Retargeting (always on)
Launches ~14 days after prospecting, once warm and hot audiences have built to sufficient scale. Single ad set combining warm + hot. 75% quest-specific creative, 25% pathway/collection-level. Runs continuously alongside prospecting.
