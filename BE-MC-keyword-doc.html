🚫 Negative Keywords — Apply to All Campaigns Day 1

  • Job seekers: life coach jobs, life coach career, life coach salary, become a life coach, life coach certification
  • Free/DIY: free self help books, free meditation app, free therapy, free affirmations, free worksheet
  • Informational only: what is manifestation, manifestation meaning, purpose definition, what is consciousness, what is law of attraction
  • Wrong audience: for kids, for teenagers, for students, reddit, youtube, download free
  • Wrong product: therapy near me, psychologist, psychiatrist, counselling
